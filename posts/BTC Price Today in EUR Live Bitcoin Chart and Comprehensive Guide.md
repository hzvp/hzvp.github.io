## How Bitcoin Works  

### Blockchain Technology  
Bitcoin's backbone is its blockchainâ€”a distributed ledger maintained by a network of nodes (computers). Miners validate these blocks by solving complex mathematical puzzles, a process known as **proof-of-work (PoW)**.
